Benjamin University (BU) is a private university located in Bukasa,
Makindye Division, in kampala,the capital city of Uganda. The University
was founded in 2019 and was launched by H.E Jesca Alpo,the Vice President of the Republic of Uganda on 11th November 2019.

THE UNIVERSITY
